Nomoxapine® Side Effects

What should I know about Nomoxapine®?

Do not take Nomoxapine® if you are allergic to it or its ingredients. In rare cases, Nomoxapine® may cause depression or suicidal thoughts.

Discuss with your healthcare provider and read the full Important Safety Information for more about possible Nomoxapine® side effects.

What are the most common side effects of Nomoxapine®?

The three most common side effects in clinical tests were:

  • nausea
  • headaches
  • rash
